Background
The builder
In 1996, at age 22, co-invented the bioresorbable "Wall Plug" interference device for ACL ligament surgery — a push-in, expansion-based fixation concept that eliminated the destructive screwing motion used worldwide. Co-founded AACL Medical SA in Lausanne, Switzerland. Filed U.S. Patent #5,989,253. The startup was unable to compete against established industry players and dissolved. Twenty-seven years later, in 2023, the global orthopedic standard finally shifted to the exact mechanism sketched in Marnaz: knotless, push-in anchors. The market is now valued at $8.3 billion. Read the full story.
Then: 15 years in corporate (Program Manager at ICRC, 45+ projects). An autoimmune disease that changed everything. Full reconversion into AI. Founded Infinity Cloud in Geneva, Switzerland. Built 65+ projects in 18 months, mostly at night. In parallel: 1,000 original music tracks on SoundCloud — every note played by hand, no generative AI.
